M.Sc, FCA, FCCA, ACTI- Chairman Board of Directors

Mr. Ogidan managed Datanomics Information Investment Limited where he provided financial and information technology education to many financial institutions including Central Bank of Nigeria, First Bank Plc and Union Bank Plc.

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD was incorporated on the 3rd March, 2021 to carry on Microfinance Banking. It was given a final licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria on the 30th September, 2021, to operate as a Tier 2 unit Microfinance Bank. The bank commenced operations on the 3rd November, 2021.
