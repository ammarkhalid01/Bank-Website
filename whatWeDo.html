ACCOUNT OPENING

Benefits

  •   Customer save and withdrawal cash at convenient time.
  •   Account attracts interest at the end of month.
  •   Account can be opened to finance target project.
  •   Account can be opened to finance certain festivity targets e.g. Xmas, Ileya, Birthday, Freedom. Etc

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN)

Individual Current Account

Benefits

  •   Access to both short and long term credit facility.
  •   Customer can guarantee other customers to get loan.

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN)

Corporate Current Account

Benefits

  •   Access to both short and long term credit facility.
  •   Customer can guarantee other customers to get loan.
  •   Access to huge credit facility.
  •   Individual/Personal guarantee of Signatories.
  •   Access to overdraft facility.

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN).
  •   Application Letter.
  •   Memorandum of Association (for group corporate account).
  •   CAC Certificate.
  •   Certificate of commencement of business.
  •   Resolution/Minutes of Meeting.

Children Account

Benefits

  •   Account can be used to get small loan facility for emergency purposes.
  •   Savings towards Future plans

Requirements

  •   BVN of parent/guardian.
  •   2 Recent passport photographs of child & parent/guardian.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN).

Student Account

Benefits

  •   Payment of school fees is guaranteed at regular time.
  •   Account can be used to get small loan facility for emergency purposes.
  •   It can be used to acquire educational materials e.g Laptop Computer, Text books etc.

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN).
  •   Admission Letter.
  •   Parent/Guardian’s guarantee/undertaking Letter.

Salary Current Account

Benefits

  •   Access to both short and long term credit facility.
  •   Customer can guarantee other customer to get loan.
  •   Access to Salary advance.
  •   Access to Overdraft facility.

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN).

Joint/Vintage/Association Account

Benefits

  •   Access to both short and long term credit facility.

  •   Account for Churhes/Monsques/Associations.

Requirements

  •   BVN
  •   2 Recent passport photographs.
  •   Recent Utility bills (PHCN, Waste Management, Water Supply).
  •   National recognized identity card (NIN).
  •   Application Letter.
  •   Constitution or Bye-Law.
  •   Decision Extract (Minutes of Meeting).
  •   Introduction Letter from Adviser/Chief Pastor/Chief Imam.

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD was incorporated on the 3rd March, 2021 to carry on Microfinance Banking. It was given a final licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria on the 30th September, 2021, to operate as a Tier 2 unit Microfinance Bank. The bank commenced operations on the 3rd November, 2021.
