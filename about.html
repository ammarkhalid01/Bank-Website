OUR
BUSINESS

We are a customer focused bank with a customer-led Ethical Policy, which sets us apart from other banks

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD was incorporated on the 3rd March, 2021 to carry on Microfinance Banking. It was given a final licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria on the 30th September, 2021, to operate as a Tier 2 unit Microfinance Bank. The bank commenced operations on the 3rd November, 2021.

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD Is a provider of Micro financial services such as savings and deposits, loans, domestic fund transfers, other financial and non-financial services to Microfinance clients in the Country.

ISUA MICROFINANCE BANK LTD is established to diminish poverty and engage youth in entrepreneurial progress in financial discipline and leading the community to become financially viable to the Local government, State and Nigeria.

OUR AIMS/OBJECTIVES

Our aim is providing financial services to the general public and to take the banking services to the doorstep of the active economically poor people, thereby better their lives. We create wealth through our innovative and technologically oriented expertise.
